River City Dance Club was founded in early 2014 in order to bring 1920's - 1930's vintage swing and blues dance culture to Chattanooga.


A list of these dances includes but is not limited to:

Lindy Hop ★ Blues ★ Charleston ★ Balboa ★ West Coast Swing


With a mission to foster a sense of community and learning, we host a variety of events, all of which have available instruction before the music starts.


Primarily, our Monday Night Lindy Hop dance is hosted conveniently in North Chattanooga. It happens all but one Monday in a year, the perfect environment to learn new skills, in practice, and just have a blast socializing and dancing.


We also host an all West Coast Swing and Blues dance on a Saturday night most months.
